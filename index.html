Your local eye-care specialist

Our mission in Albert Park is to provide the local community with quality optometric care. To do this, a high level of professional continuing education is maintained and we have a fully-equipped consultation room for thorough eye examinations. This includes a perimeter (visual field tester), a digital retinal camera and an OCT (Optical Coherence Tomographer) scanner for the detection and monitoring of conditions including glaucoma, macular degeneration and diabetic retinopathy. The earlier eye diseases are detected, the easier they are to treat.

Our service extends beyond the sale of glasses and we are always happy to make frame adjustments and minor repairs to keep your glasses in the best possible working condition.

Catherine began Catherine Young Optometrist in 1998 and has become an integral part of the Albert Park village.

Services

Our thorough eye examinations include:

  • Retinal photography
  • Optical Coherence topography (scans the retinal layers)
  • Intra-ocular pressures (part of glaucoma testing)
  • Visual fields (on indication)
  • External eye examinations

Eye examinations sometimes involve eye drops to dilate the pupils which results in photophobia (light sensitivity). Patients over the age of 65 are more likely to require dilation drops and are advised to bring sunglasses to their appointment.
