Your leading optometrist & eye care specialist

Vision is a personal experience, and everyone sees the world with their own unique perspective. When you visit one of our eight compassionate Eye Concepts locations, we are dedicated to providing the best possible eye care solution to suit your visual expectations and lifestyle requirements. Our practices are family-friendly, and we welcome customers of all ages.


Based on the success of our first family practice, Fairfield, we have since expanded to other locations around Sydney and Melbourne, including Burwood, Merrylands, North Sydney, Hurstville, Oran Park, Narellan, Edmondson Park, Wetherill Park and our very first store in Melbourne - Southland. We are proud to be operating in these locations with the same commitment to delivering ultimate customer satisfaction and top quality eye health care. Don’t take our word for it – pay us a visit and see for yourself!

Catherine Young

Catherine began practicing optometry in 1989. She moved to Perth after her graduation and worked there for 5 years before returning to Melbourne with the idea of starting her own practice. Two years later in 1995, Catherine Young Optometrist was established in Albert Park.

The practice

Our mission in Albert Park is to provide the local community with quality optometric care. To do this, a high level of professional continuing education is maintained and we have a fully-equipped consultation room for thorough eye examinations. This includes a perimeter (visual field tester), a digital retinal camera and an OCT (Optical Coherence Tomographer) scanner for the detection and monitoring of conditions including glaucoma, macular degeneration and diabetic retinopathy. The earlier eye diseases are detected, the easier they are to treat.


Our service extends beyond the sale of glasses and we are always happy to make frame adjustments and minor repairs to keep your glasses in the best possible working condition.
