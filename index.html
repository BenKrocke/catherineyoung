Your local eye-care specialist

Since the beginning of Catherine Young Optometrist in 1998, our mission has been to provide the Albert Park community with quality optometric care. To do this, we maintain a high level of professional education and a warm approach to customer service. We understand the importance of a quality examination, followed by advice tailored to your needs.

Our fully-equipped consultation room allows our staff to examine, detect and monitor eye conditions such as glaucoma, macular degeneration, and diabetic retinopathy. Furthermore, we prescribe, fit, and supply glasses and contact lenses, choosing the best solution for you.

The earlier eye diseases are detected, the easier they are to treat.

Products

Services

Our services include, but are not limited to:

  • Thorough eye examination for visual disorders
  • Prescription, fitting and supply of glasses and contact lenses
  • Management of vision problems and provision of eye health care and advice

Our fully-equipped consultation room contains a perimeter (visual field tester), a digital retinal camera and an OCT (Optical Coherence Tomographer) scanner. These are used for detecting and monitoring conditions such as glaucoma, macular degeneration and diabetic retinopathy.

Daniel Adler

Daniel has been at Catherine Young Optometrists since 2015 and is a behavioural optometrist: he has a special interest in children’s vision and works with parents to optimize children’s visual outcomes.

Children's Vision Centre

Good vision is vital for a child’s educational, physical and social development. Approximately 1 in 4 Australian children suffer from an undetected vision problem. It is, therefore, essential to be aware of the possible signs of a vision problem to give every child the best chance of reaching their full learning potential.

At Albert Park Children’s Vision Centre, we understand how crucial early detection of vision problems can be. Through our children’s vision campaign, we aim to educate teachers and parents about the importance of regular eye examinations. Albert Park Children’s Vision Centre was established in 2004, and our behavioural optometrist, Daniel, is available Monday afternoons and Wednesdays for appointments.
